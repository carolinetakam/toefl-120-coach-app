# TOEFL 120 Coach Launch Campaign

## Positioning

TOEFL 120 Coach helps learners move from random practice to focused prep with daily planning, realistic tasks, review loops, and clear next actions.

Do not claim guaranteed scores, ETS affiliation, official scoring, or score increases without real evidence.

Safe disclaimer:

> TOEFL is a registered trademark of ETS. TOEFL 120 Coach is an independent preparation tool and is not affiliated with or endorsed by ETS.

## Audience

First market: Korea.

- Korean students applying to universities, exchange programs, graduate school, or international schools
- Retakers stuck below a target TOEFL score
- Budget-conscious learners who need structure before expensive tutoring
- Parents and study-abroad counselors looking for visible practice progress

Do not launch broad/global until the Korean beta loop proves retention, support load, and scoring clarity.

## 7-Day Teaser Plan

1. Korea-specific problem: TOEFL prep feels scattered even when learners study every day.
2. Poll: hardest section to improve: speaking, writing, listening, reading.
3. Education: random prep vs targeted daily prep.
4. Product glimpse: daily plan and mini mock task.
5. Skill focus: speaking structure under time pressure.
6. Beta invite: 5-20 Korean learners, practice-signal disclaimer.
7. Launch: closed beta page, not paid early access yet.
