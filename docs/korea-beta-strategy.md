# Korea-First Beta Strategy

## Market Choice

Start with Korea before broad global launch.

Reasons:

- TOEFL demand is concentrated and familiar.
- Learners and parents already pay for English outcomes.
- Speaking and writing anxiety are strong pain points.
- Dense communities make founder-led beta recruiting possible.
- Korea timezone makes support and interviews practical.

## First ICP

Korean TOEFL learners preparing for:

- study abroad
- exchange programs
- international schools
- graduate school admissions
- retakes after missing a target score

Exclude for now:

- learners who need official score prediction
- institutions requiring admin dashboards
- parents asking for guaranteed score improvement
- users needing full Korean-language product support

## Positioning

English:

> A private TOEFL practice coach that tells you what to fix every day.

Korean:

> 매일 무엇을 고쳐야 하는지 알려주는 개인 TOEFL 연습 코치.

Safety line:

> Practice signals only. Not an official TOEFL score and not affiliated with ETS.

## Beta Offer

Free closed beta for 5-20 learners.

Beta promise:

- daily practice plan
- mini mock baseline
- speaking/writing structure feedback
- review loop for repeated mistakes
- founder-led support

No paid launch until:

- production Clerk keys are live
- support email is configured
- first cohort completes week 1
- scoring confusion is below critical threshold
- privacy/terms/support pages are live

## Recruiting Channels

Use founder-owned, low-cost outreach first:

- Korean TOEFL study cafes and communities
- university exchange-program groups
- international-school parent networks
- LinkedIn posts targeting Korean study-abroad counselors
- short-form video about TOEFL study planning mistakes

Do not run paid ads until retention and support load are understood.

## Week 1 Success Metrics

For each learner:

- onboarding completed
- diagnostic completed
- first mini mock completed
- at least three practice days
- second mini mock completed
- learner can explain their top blocker
- no unresolved P0/P1 support issue

Product threshold to expand:

- 70% complete the first mini mock
- 50% return for day-two practice
- 30% complete the five-day loop
- fewer than 20% report scoring language as misleading

## Launch Copy

Korean short post:

> TOEFL 공부를 열심히 하는데 매일 무엇을 해야 할지 헷갈린다면, TOEFL 120 Coach 베타를 테스트해 주세요. 공식 점수 예측이 아니라, 매일 고쳐야 할 약점을 찾고 연습 루틴을 만드는 도구입니다.

English short post:

> Building a Korea-first TOEFL practice coach for learners who need a clearer daily plan, especially for speaking and writing. Closed beta first. Practice signals only, not official scoring.

## Expansion Sequence

1. Korea closed beta.
2. Korea paid early-access cohort.
3. Japan/Taiwan/Vietnam English-first beta.
4. Counselor/school partnerships after support workflow is proven.
